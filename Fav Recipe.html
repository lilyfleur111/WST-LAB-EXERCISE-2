Suzanne's Recipe for Sinigang!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb pork ribs, cut into serving pieces
  • 8 cups water
  • 1 medium onion, quartered
  • 2 medium tomatoes, quartered
  • 2 cups fresh kangkong (water spinach) leaves
  • 1 cup radish, sliced
  • 1 cup string beans, cut into 2-inch lengths
  • 1 cup okra
  • 2 pieces long green chili peppers
  • 1 pack sinigang mix (tamarind soup base)
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, bring water to a boil.
  2. Add the pork ribs and simmer until tender, about 1 hour.
  3. Skim off any scum that rises to the surface.
  4. Add onions and tomatoes, and simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Add radish and simmer for another 3 minutes.
  6. Stir in kangkong, string beans, okra, and long green chili peppers. Simmer for 3-5 minutes or until vegetables are tender.
  7. Add sinigang mix and stir until fully dissolved.
  8. Season with salt to taste.
  9. Remove from heat and serve hot.
